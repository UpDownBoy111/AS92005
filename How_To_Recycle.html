How and Why You Should Recycle

Environmental Benefit: Environmental benefit is important in recycling since we do not have to start from scratch we creating object. Like metal or paper, maybe even plastic. Protection of Wildlife: By recycling, we can protect the turtles, they eat plastic bags and straws, which we are polluting into our oceans. Reduces Pollution: With more recycling we can reduce pollution by reusing materials wasted.

When it comes to recycling, different rules apply for residential versus commercial, and then again depending on location. The general form on how to recyle is after collection, recyclables are sent to a recovery facility to be sorted, cleaned, and processed into materials that can be used in manufacturing.
